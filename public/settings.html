About System
Car Connect Lusaka Rental Management System

Version 1.0

This car rental management system was developed to help rental businesses efficiently manage their fleet, track rentals, and handle important reminders.

Features:
  • Car inventory management
  • Rental tracking and management
  • Automated reminders for important dates
  • Document management for vehicles and rentals
  • Data backup and export capabilities
  • Mobile-friendly design
